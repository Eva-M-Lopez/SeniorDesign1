## About the FallRisk: SafeFall Coach App
A mobile application designed for older adults, caregivers, and healthcare professionals to educate and train users on safe falling techniques, fall recovery, and fall prevention strategies by using interactive modules, pose estimation, and real-time feedback
  
## Project Motivation
- Falls are costly, which often results in serious health consequences and disability
- Teaching safe movement and fall strategies can siginificantly reduce the risk of inhury by helping individuals minimize impact forces during a fall
